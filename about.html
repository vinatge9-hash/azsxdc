Our Story

Niyantri Coffee began as a tiny, sunlit corner in Hyderabad where baristas crafted each cup with patience and care. We draw inspiration from artisan coffee culture—slow, thoughtful, and beautifully imperfect. Our space blends warm browns, creams, and gold accents to create a sanctuary where students can focus, connect, and dream a little bigger over a perfect pour.

Our Craft

We source ethically, roast in small batches, and design every element—from furniture to typography—to invite calm, focus, and delight. Expect a study-friendly vibe with soft lighting, cozy seating, and a soundtrack that whispers, not shouts.
